| Attribute | Augury |

| **HQ / geo** | New York + Haifa (Israel); US-remote roles exist |

**The matchable "shape" (what to look for elsewhere):**
1. **Industrial / manufacturing / physical-operations** domain (plants, factories, field, heavy asset ops).
2. **AI-native, ideally agentic** — building copilots/agents, LLM eval, production AI reliability, or applied ML on operational data.
3. **B2B enterprise SaaS** with a real product team to lead (not solo IC).
4. **Complex/fragmented data systems** — unifying disconnected industrial sources (historians, CMMS, sensors, CAD, ERP).
5. **Remote-friendly, non-Spanish employer**, US/EU async acceptable.

Mikhail is unusually credible wherever **#1 (industrial personas) and #2 (agentic AI) intersect** —
that intersection is his headline, so weight companies that need both over pure-SaaS or pure-ML shops.
